Honeydew Valley

Controls

Movement: WASD

Action: Spacebar

Inventory: 1/2/3/4/5

Exit: ESC

Drop item: Backspace

Some stuff to know

Items sell for half their buy value

Plants will die when water level reaches 0

Plants wont grow under a certain water level

You will die when your hunger level reaches 0

You need to add one veggie and one fish to stove
